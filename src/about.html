ABOUT SCR, INC

SCR is a locally owned and operated general contracting company specializing in insurance recovery restoration, including wind, hail, smoke, fire, and flood damage. Our team of former insurance professionals continues to hold an all-line adjusters license and are very knowledgeable of the rights and responsibilities contained in your insurance policy. We go to great lengths in selecting the most experienced and professional representatives that reflect our company values and integrity.

Since our founding in 1998, SCR has had thousands of satisfied customers (commercial and residential) on everything from small homes to multi-million dollar corporations. We are fully licensed and insured for your protection and ours, and upon request, we will list you as the certificate holder on our liability insurance.

Additionally, SCR has been fully vetted with an extensive (business and personal) background check and has qualified as a member of The Good Contractors List. How does this help you? They offer YOU a $10,000 guarantee on Quality and fully stand behind our workmanship! Of course, you will never need this guarantee because our work is superb, but it’s just an additional layer of protection for you, the homeowner/business owner! https://thegoodcontractorslist.com/list/roofing/scr-inc

Meet The Founder and Owner - Ken Nelson

Ken Nelson, founder and owner of SCR Inc. has a wealth of first-hand knowledge in the industry. Before starting SCR in 1998, Ken was a catastrophic adjustor, working through six major hurricanes that pounded the coast as well as people's homes. Ken gained the knowledge and experience necessary to develop a successful and competitive edge. Ken and his son Donnie have built a strong team at SCR on the foundation of "integrity first, service before self, and excellence in all we do."

Our Team
