STORM DAMAGE RESTORATION AND ROOFING SERVICES YOU CAN RELY ON SINCE 1998

SCR, Inc. is a locally owned and operated business serving the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex since 1998. We specialize in insurance recovery restoration including wind, hail, water, smoke and fire damage. We are not a public adjusting firm and do not operate in that capacity, however our team of former insurance adjusters are very knowledgeable and experienced in the insurance process and hold all-lines adjusters license, issued by the state of Texas. We assist our customers with the sometimes troublesome insurance process by explaining the following steps: filing your claim, meeting your adjuster, all necessary paperwork after the work is complete, and the claim appeal process if necessary. Call us today at 972-839-6834 for more information.

Commercial: Warehouses, shopping centers, sports arenas, and apartment complexes. There is no task too big for us to handle.

Our Network of Professionals Will Gladly Assist You with Your Claim Including:

  • How to file your claim
  • Meeting your adjuster
  • Providing all necessary documents to determine the scope/amount of damages
  • Explaining the claims appeal process (when necessary) including arbitration, mediation, and appraisal.

  • Obtaining mortgage endorsements, as your mortgage company will be listed as an additional loss payee on large claims.
